Our Plantation Programs

School Plantation Drive

Engaging students in environmental conservation by organizing tree plantation drives in schools and educational institutions.

  • 5,200 trees planted in schools
  • 120 schools participated
  • 15,000 students involved

Urban Greening

Transforming urban spaces by planting trees in parks, roadsides, and community areas to improve air quality.

  • 6,800 trees in urban areas
  • 25 parks developed
  • 50 km of roadside plantation

Forest Restoration

Restoring degraded forest areas and creating new forest patches to preserve biodiversity and prevent soil erosion.

  • 3,420 trees in forest areas
  • 15 forest patches restored
  • 200 hectares covered
