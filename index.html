Our Mission

At JivanJyoti Foundation, our mission is to safeguard the world's forests and their invaluable ecosystems. We are dedicated to promoting sustainable practices, raising awareness about deforestation, and empowering communities to become stewards of their natural environment. Every tree planted, every forest protected, contributes to a healthier planet for generations to come.

Forest Conservation

Protecting existing forests from logging and degradation.

Reforestation Efforts

Planting new trees to restore deforested areas and combat climate change.

Wildlife Protection

Safeguarding the diverse species that call these forests home.
